How it works

Simple 4-step booking

📋 Booking steps

1

Check availability

Fill in the form below or send a WhatsApp message with your check‑in and check‑out dates and number of guests. Your host will confirm availability.

2

Receive confirmation

Your host will reply confirming the room type, rate and availability — usually within a few hours.

3

Pay deposit (USD 10)

Pay a non‑refundable USD 10 deposit per room via PayPal or QR code scan to secure your booking.

4

Arrive & pay balance

Check in from 2:00 PM. Pay the remaining balance in cash to your host at LP Residence on arrival.
