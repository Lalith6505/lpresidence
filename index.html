About us & reviews

Family‑run guest house

LP Residence is a friendly, family‑run guest house offering quiet, independent rooms for rent in the heart of Kottawa town. You stay in a peaceful residential street while still being just a short walk from local shops, temples and daily life.

Guests enjoy comfortable bedrooms, free on‑site parking and high‑speed Wi‑Fi included as standard. All main meals can be arranged on request for an additional charge, so you can choose between eating out or enjoying home‑style Sri Lankan food at the property.

For travellers, LP Residence is very conveniently located close to the Makumbura Multimodal Transport Hub and the Southern Expressway entrance, making it easy to reach Colombo and other parts of the island. Airport pickup and drop‑off can be arranged in advance, so you can arrive and depart without stress.

Whether you are visiting family, travelling for work or exploring Sri Lanka, we offer a simple, affordable and homely place to stay with hosts who are always happy to help.
